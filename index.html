Why Jovair Exists

Comparing flights paid with cash versus flights paid with miles is a pain.

You end up searching the same route, the same dates, and the same flights across multiple sites — just to have prices or mileage costs change while you’re comparing.

Jovair puts both options side by side so you can see which one actually makes sense.

How Jovair Works

  1. You enter your route, dates, and cabin once.
  2. Jovair compares available cash prices and miles options.
  3. We highlight the better deal, and you decide how to book.
